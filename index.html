About Breakdown Buddy LLC

Founded in 2015, Breakdown Buddy LLC started with a simple mission: to provide Sacramento residents with reliable, professional roadside assistance when they need it most. What began as a small operation with just two tow trucks has grown into a trusted name in emergency towing and roadside services. Our founder, with over 20 years of experience in the automotive industry, recognized the need for a service that combined speed, professionalism, and fair pricing. Today, we operate a fleet of modern, well-maintained vehicles and employ certified technicians who are committed to delivering exceptional service every single day.

At Breakdown Buddy LLC, we believe that being stranded on the side of the road shouldn't be a stressful experience. Our core values—reliability, integrity, and customer care—guide everything we do. We're not just a towing company; we're your roadside partner. We treat every customer with respect, handle every vehicle with care, and always put your safety and satisfaction first. Whether you're a regular commuter or a one-time customer, you can count on us to be there when you need us most, providing professional service that you can trust.

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about our services? We've got answers. Check out our most commonly asked questions below.

What areas do you service?

Breakdown Buddy LLC primarily serves Sacramento and the surrounding areas, including Carmichael, Folsom, Elk Grove, Rancho Cordova, and Citrus Heights. We also provide long-distance towing services to other parts of Northern California. If you're unsure whether your location is within our service area, please contact us directly and we'll be happy to help.

How quickly will you arrive?

Our average response time is 20-30 minutes in most areas of Sacramento. During peak hours or in remote areas, response times may be slightly longer. We maintain a fleet of vehicles strategically positioned throughout our service area to ensure fast response times. When you call, we'll provide you with an estimated time of arrival so you know when to expect help.

Do you accept all types of vehicles?

Yes! We tow all types of vehicles including cars, trucks, SUVs, motorcycles, RVs, and more. Our fleet includes different tow trucks equipped to handle various vehicle sizes and weights. Whether you're driving a compact sedan or a heavy-duty pickup truck, we have the equipment and expertise to safely tow your vehicle. If you have questions about a specific vehicle type, feel free to call us.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), debit cards, cash, and mobile payment options like Apple Pay and Google Pay. We can process payments on-site when our technician arrives, or you can pay over the phone before service. Our transparent pricing means no surprises—we'll quote you a price upfront, and that's what you'll pay.

Are your services available at night and on weekends?

Absolutely! Breakdown Buddy LLC operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, including nights, weekends, and holidays. Roadside emergencies don't follow a schedule, so neither do we. Whether you need help at 3 AM on a Sunday or during rush hour on a Wednesday, we're here to help. Our pricing is the same regardless of the time of day or day of the week.
